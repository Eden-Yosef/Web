About us...

Our site is free and intended for students to help them keep track of their grades.
On our website you can add the courses you took during your studies and the grade you received for them,
after that the average grades of all the courses will be calculated by the credit points and the grade of each entered course.
At any given time you will be able to know the average of your grades, which courses you have taken,
the grades you have received and of course add and delete courses as needed. We hope that our website will help you in tracking your studies.
